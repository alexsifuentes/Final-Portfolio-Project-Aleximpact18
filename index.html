My Mission

My Mission is to having coding available to everyone, whether they have experience with coding languages or not. I believe that no one should not believe that they cannot code because they don't have the capacity or the knowledge to do it. With enough practice and help from peers, you can code and gain that knowledge to teach others.  

I hope to apply my knowledge of HTML, CSS, and BootStrap to the tech world and help those that are struggling or want to learn more about each of the three languages. I firmly believe that knowing how to develop a website, even if one is not in the tech industry or did not/are not currently studying in a degree in the tech field, it will expand your job opportunities and/or can be used as a side hooby that one would enjoy.  

I plan to be in the tech industry, either in the IT field or a career that includes devloping for the web or software development. As I am going abroad to become an intern in Tokyo, Japan, I plan to use what I learned to the test and further develop my web development skills, as well as stregthening my skills in html, CSS, Javascript, and hope to develop new skills throughout my time as an intern.
